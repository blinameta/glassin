About

Our work experience includes a wide range of projects using specialized materials, complex geometry, new structural and mechanical system designs. Glassin facade, curtainwall and skylight systems combine innovative design with high-end materials and performance. Glassin has over 20 years of experience in the design, engineering, fabrication, assembly and erection of custom facade systems, providing complete design-build services to the construction marketplace.

Our Beginning

'KON-AL' was the first company that brought aluminum trends in the country. It was founded in 1992 and it stayed active until 1999, when it changed its name to "Metaj." "Metaj" brought innovations to the market by adding plastic and glass products to its product assortments thus guaranteeing the production of reliable and qualitative product variety.

GlassIN brand

Expanding the product collection resulted in the creation of GlassIN brand, a bigger company dominated by modern minimalism, accuracy, quality and visual refinement in all products of all types and materials. GlassIN has a significant place in all the buildings with architectural identity in the country. Constituted by experienced engineers in the field architecture, construction and electronics, GlassIN achieves the production of maximum quality products.
